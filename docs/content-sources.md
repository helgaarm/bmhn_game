## Publication rule

Before adding a high-impact technical statement, record its exact claim, authoritative current URL, retrieval date, content owner, review status, and affected quest/content IDs. Report material may shape learning design but does not by itself establish current Helsenorge, HelseID, FHIR, SMART, security, privacy, or integration requirements.
