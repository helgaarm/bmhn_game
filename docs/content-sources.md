## Publication rule

Before adding a high-impact technical statement, record its exact claim, authoritative current URL, retrieval date, content owner, version, exceptions, required approvers, recheck date and affected quest/content IDs. The current register is documented in `docs/production-rules.md`. Report material may shape learning design but does not by itself establish current Helsenorge, HelseID, FHIR, SMART, security, privacy, or integration requirements.
